Who We Are

Second Stage Management Services Ltd. is a full service, technical production company specializing in corporate events. We have been creating and fine-tuning multimedia presentations for companies large and small, local and international since 1991. With over 50 years of combined experience, our partners take great pride in our work and it reflects in the long history we have enjoyed with our clients.

Our Services

  • Venue inspection, selection and liaison
  • Event layout and space allocation
  • Technical and creative requirements
  • Video , photography and computer content
  • Stage design and construction
  • Subcontractor selection and supervision
  • On-site technical direction
  • Event stage management

The Difference

At Second Stage Management Services Ltd. we are not just an equipment supplier, but a trusted addition to your event team. Our partners have the ability to evaluate your needs then provide the appropriate solution to your event, not simply sell you what we have on our shelves. Imagination is our only limitation, and you will never hear us say “that’s good enough”. We believe it never is.
