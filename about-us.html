About Us

Second Stage Management Services Ltd. recognizes with gratitude how fortunate we are as individuals and as a company. As a result of our good fortune, we have undertaken the support of several charities, the largest being Dress for Success® Vancouver. We donate time, creative endeavors and AV equipment to all their fundraising events. It is a small way we can repay our “Corporate Karma”.
